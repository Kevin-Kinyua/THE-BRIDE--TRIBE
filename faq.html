Frequently Asked Questions



About Us

We are an online-only listing service. This means we do not consign or buy gowns, do not have a store location, and every dress and item listing is being sold by a real person just like you. The Bride Tribe connects buyers and sellers of wedding items who then transact directly with each other so that the buyer saves, and the seller recoups as much investment as possible. Take advantage of a free TBT account, giving you access to secure messaging, saved search alerts and a dedicated support team.

Some general safety tips when buying online:

  • • Never send money without verifying the seller
  • • Use our secure messaging to chat to potential buyers and keep your contact details private
  • • Ask Questions. Confirm the authenticity and condition of your item. Ask the seller lots of questions about its current condition, the size & alterations done, the color, and ask if they offer a return policy and HOW the return policy works.
  • • Ask for Pictures. SO IMPORTANT! You need to see the dress in its current condition as of today. An honest seller won’t mind – a scammer will make up a reason they can’t send you pictures of the item as it looks right now in their hands.
  • • View wedding dresses in person- TBT is an online marketplace and we encourage face-to-face, local trading. For personal ease and safety, always ensure you take someone with you, or if you are inviting someone into your home ask to see personal identification first.
  • • Use common sense- If something sounds too good to be true, it probably is.


Always Be Careful, Let Us Know If You See Suspicious Behavior! The Bride Tribe is all about making buying and selling safe for everyone. The main thing to remember when you’re buying your used wedding dress online is to be careful. An honest seller is going to be just as cautious as you, but they will be giving you the right answers and not setting off alarms. If any of the above happens to you, or if you’re ever suspicious of an e-mail, provide us with the email exchange at support@thebridetribe.com and we will block this person from selling on The Bride Tribe if we feel they are a scammer. Just take the time to investigate and ask the right questions to ensure that your transaction will be quick and easy!
